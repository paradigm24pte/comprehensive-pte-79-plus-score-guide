Understanding the Pearson Academic PTE

The Pearson Academic PTE is a computer-based test that assesses a candidate’s ability to communicate effectively in English. The exam evaluates four primary skills: speaking, listening, reading, and writing. One of the main advantages of PTE is its automated scoring technology, which ensures objective and consistent evaluation.

Why Choose Pearson Academic PTE?

Objective and Reliable Assessment: The automated scoring system ensures impartial evaluation.
Quick Results: Results are available within a few business days, making it ideal for those in a hurry.
Globally Recognized: The PTE is accepted by educational institutions, government bodies, and organizations worldwide.

Key Strategies for Scoring 79+ in PTE

Scoring 79+ in the PTE requires a blend of hard work, practice, and strategic preparation. Below are section-specific tips to help you achieve your target score.

Speaking Section Tips and Tricks

The speaking section is often considered one of the most challenging components of the PTE exam. It evaluates your ability to communicate fluently and accurately in spoken English.

Fluency and Pronunciation

Begin with a rising tone and end with a falling tone to sound more authentic.
If you make an error while speaking, keep going; pausing may affect your fluency score.
Practice tongue twisters daily to improve your pronunciation and fluency.

Repeat Sentence

Close your eyes while listening to the sentence and memorize it.
Start speaking as soon as the clip ends.
Focus on capturing the keywords in the sentence.

Describe Image

Begin your description with phrases like “depicts,” “illustrates,” or “demonstrates.”
Identify the chart or image type and use appropriate directional language.
Avoid completing recorded sentences to maintain fluency.

Re-tell Lecture

Focus on main keywords and ideas from the lecture.
Use phrases like “the speaker mentions” or “the professor says.”
Capture the best 2-4 sentences from the lecture and summarize them effectively.

Reading Section Tips and Tricks

The reading section evaluates your comprehension and analytical skills. Here are some essential tips:

Fill in the Blanks

Identify the main message of the passage and use keywords to predict missing words.
Pay close attention to frequency words and tense.
Apply the rule of elimination to narrow down your options.

Re-order Paragraphs

Start by identifying the headline or main idea of the passage.
Look for sentences that contain pronouns or reference earlier information.
Rephrase any contradictory phrases to help identify the correct order.

Multiple Choice, Single Answer

Focus on understanding the primary idea of the passage.
Utilize the process of elimination to find the correct answer.
Read the question first to know what information to focus on while reading the passage.

Writing Section Tips and Strategies

The writing section tests your ability to express ideas clearly and concisely.

Summarize Written Text

Summarize the text in your own words while maintaining the essence of the passage.
Use a variety of vocabulary and sentence structures to demonstrate language proficiency.
Be mindful of punctuation and write in the third person.

Essay Writing

Stick to a clear essay structure: introduction, body paragraphs, and conclusion.
Support your arguments with examples, reasons, and statistics.
Keep your essay between 200-300 words for optimal results.

Listening Section Tips and Best Practices

The listening section evaluates your ability to comprehend spoken English in various contexts.

Stay Focused on Audio

Write down keywords as you listen to the audio to support your answers.
Focus on understanding the speaker’s main theme and repeated words.

Fill in the Blanks

Use the notepad provided to jot down important points.
Pay attention to singular/plural forms and article usage.

Write from Dictation

Practice typing directly into the computer to improve speed and accuracy.
Be mindful of spelling, punctuation, and singular/plural forms.

Final Tips for Success in PTE 79+ Score

Practice within time limits to simulate real exam conditions.
Choose preparation materials that include frequently repeated questions to familiarize yourself with the exam format.
Focus on improving your typing speed and accuracy, as many sections require fast and efficient typing.
Achieving a 79+ score in the PTE requires dedication, practice, and a solid strategy across all sections of the exam. Following these tips and practicing consistently will help you confidently achieve your desired score.
